Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0deg Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of the year than the norhtern hemisphere. This is due to the tilt of the earth (earth's wobble) at this time of the year.
